About

Anil Pharande is the visionary founder of Pharande Spaces and one of the most respected real estate leaders in Pune and PCMC. With over three decades of experience in the real estate sector, he has played a significant role in shaping the region’s residential and commercial landscape through integrated townships, premium residential communities, luxury villas, and customer-centric developments.

Known for his focus on quality construction, strategic location selection, infrastructure-led planning, and long-term value creation, Anil Pharande has consistently delivered projects that combine modern architecture, lifestyle amenities, green spaces, and excellent connectivity. His developments are designed to meet the evolving needs of homebuyers while contributing to the growth of emerging real estate corridors across Pune and PCMC.

Beyond real estate development, Anil Pharande is a respected industry leader who has actively contributed to the advancement of the real estate sector. Having served as President of CREDAI Pune, he has participated in industry discussions, urban development initiatives, and infrastructure-focused planning efforts. Through visionary leadership, innovation, and a commitment to customer satisfaction, he continues to contribute to the development of future-ready communities and the ongoing urban transformation of Pune and PCMC. His journey as a first-generation entrepreneur reflects determination, strategic thinking, and a lasting contribution to regional real estate development.
